# NovaStore Customer Support Escalation and Response Policy

## Automated support scope
The automated assistant may answer general questions about orders, shipping, returns, payments, account access, and published company policies.

## When to escalate to a human agent
Escalation is required when:
- The customer reports fraud or unauthorized activity.
- The request involves a high-value order.
- The customer disputes a previous decision.
- The issue cannot be resolved using the knowledge base.
- The customer has contacted support more than twice about the same unresolved issue.
- Personal identity verification is required.
- The customer explicitly asks to speak with a human agent.

## Response targets
- General questions: immediate automated response
- Standard agent cases: within **4 business hours**
- Urgent security or fraud cases: within **1 business hour**
- Carrier investigations: initial update within **2 business days**

## Safety and privacy
Agents and automated systems must not request full passwords, complete payment-card numbers, or one-time verification codes. Sensitive customer data must be masked in logs and excluded from the vector database.

## Uncertain answers
If the retrieved information is incomplete, conflicting, or outdated, the assistant must state that it cannot confirm the answer and escalate instead of guessing.
