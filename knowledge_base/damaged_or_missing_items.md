# NovaStore Damaged, Incorrect, or Missing Items Policy

## Reporting window
Customers should report a damaged, incorrect, or missing item within **48 hours** of delivery.

## Required information
The customer should provide:
- Order number
- Description of the issue
- Clear photos of the item and packaging
- Photo of the shipping label when available

## Damaged items
After verification, NovaStore may offer a replacement, refund, or store credit. The customer should keep the item and packaging until support provides instructions.

## Incorrect items
If the wrong product was delivered, NovaStore provides return instructions and covers reasonable return shipping. A replacement is sent after the issue is confirmed, subject to stock availability.

## Missing items
For multi-item orders, customers should check whether the order was split into multiple shipments. If the package is marked delivered but is missing, the customer should check nearby locations and wait up to **24 hours** before reporting it.

## Escalation
Cases involving high-value items, repeated delivery claims, or suspected package tampering must be escalated for manual review.
