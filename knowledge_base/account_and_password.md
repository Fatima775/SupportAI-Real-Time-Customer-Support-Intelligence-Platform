# NovaStore Account Access and Password Recovery

## Password reset
Customers can reset a password by selecting **Forgot Password** on the sign-in page. A verification link is sent to the registered email address. The link expires after **15 minutes** and can be used only once.

## Verification email not received
Customers should:
1. Check spam or junk folders.
2. Confirm that the email address was entered correctly.
3. Wait up to **5 minutes**.
4. Request one additional reset email.

Repeated requests may invalidate earlier links.

## Locked accounts
An account may be temporarily locked after several unsuccessful sign-in attempts. The lock is normally removed after **30 minutes**. Customers should avoid repeated attempts during the lock period.

## Email or phone changes
Changes to registered contact details require identity verification. Support agents must not ask customers to provide full passwords, complete card numbers, or one-time verification codes.

## Suspicious access
If unauthorized access is suspected, the customer should reset the password immediately, review recent orders, and contact support. The case should be escalated to the security team if an unknown order or account change is found.
