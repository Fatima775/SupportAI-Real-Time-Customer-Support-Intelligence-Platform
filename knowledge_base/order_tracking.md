# NovaStore Order Tracking and Status Guide

## Order statuses
- **Pending:** Payment or order verification is incomplete.
- **Processing:** Payment is confirmed and the order is being prepared.
- **Packed:** The package is ready for carrier pickup.
- **Shipped:** The carrier has received the package.
- **Out for Delivery:** Delivery is expected that day.
- **Delivered:** The carrier recorded delivery.
- **Cancelled:** The order will not be shipped.
- **Returned:** The package was received back by NovaStore.

## Tracking information
Tracking information is usually available within **24 hours** after the status changes to Shipped. A tracking number may show no movement until the carrier performs the first scan.

## No tracking update
If tracking has not changed for **48 hours**, the customer may contact support. Support can open a carrier investigation if the shipment is beyond the estimated delivery period.

## Split shipments
Items from the same order may be shipped separately. Each shipment may have a different tracking number and delivery date.
