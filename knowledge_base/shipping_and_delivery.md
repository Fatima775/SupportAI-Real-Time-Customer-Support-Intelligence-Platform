# NovaStore Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Processing time
Orders are normally processed within **1–2 business days** after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

## Delivery estimates
- Standard delivery: **3–7 business days**
- Express delivery: **1–3 business days**
- Remote-area delivery: may require an additional **2–4 business days**

Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by weather, carrier delays, customs checks, or incorrect address details.

## Shipping address changes
Customers may request an address change only before the order status changes to **Shipped**. Once the package has been handed to the carrier, the address cannot be changed by NovaStore.

## Delayed delivery
A package is considered delayed when it has not arrived **2 business days** after the estimated delivery date. Customers should first check the tracking page. If there is no movement for **48 hours**, customer support can open a carrier investigation.

## Delivery confirmation
The carrier may mark the order as delivered before the package is physically received. Customers should allow up to **24 hours**, check nearby locations, and ask household members or building reception before reporting the package as missing.
